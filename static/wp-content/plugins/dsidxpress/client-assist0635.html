Fabulous Architecture! Authentic Custom Victorian beauty with 1,230 square foot FIVE car detached garage. Designed and constructed to look like it was built in the Queen Anne period, but actually constructed in 1987. Home overlooks Lake Park with its playground, Scout Cabin, picnic areas, green grass, and beautiful mature trees. The entrance has a custom Herringbone pattern flooring with New England antique brick, intricate porches with carved pillars and hand-turned Acanthus Leaves. The home has been built with premium craftsmanship, wood siding exterior, a chandelier in the entry way imported from France, breathtaking staircase and wood floors. A gourmet kitchen overlooks the Family Room and inviting back porch. Detached 5 car garage with workshop area and huge lot. The back yard has beautiful landscaping which simply must be seen. It features a meandering stream and black bottom spa designed to look like a reflecting pond.
